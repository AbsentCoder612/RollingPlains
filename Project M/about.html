About Us

Rolling Plains is a trusted name in commercial and residential cleaning, built on 13 years of hands-on experience and a commitment to doing the job right. In 2025, we proudly rebranded to better reflect who we are today—modern, dependable, and focused on delivering consistent, high-quality results. We serve both businesses and homeowners with customized cleaning solutions designed to fit each space’s unique needs. From offices and commercial facilities to homes of all sizes, our team approaches every project with care, attention to detail, and professionalism. At Rolling Plains, we believe a clean environment makes a difference. Our experience, reliability, and renewed vision drive us to create spaces that look better, feel better, and work better for the people who use them every day.
